# VLX-500 Product Brief (Draft v0.9)

**Internal - Confidential | Product team, March 2026**

The VLX-500 is Veltrix's next-generation AMR, targeted for launch in
**Q4 2026** at list price **$56,000** (RaaS: $2,250/robot/month).

## Headline improvements over VLX-400

| Spec | VLX-400 (current) | VLX-500 (target) |
|---|---|---|
| Payload | 600 kg | 950 kg |
| Top speed | 1.8 m/s | 2.4 m/s |
| Battery | 8h swappable Li-ion | 11h fast-charge LFP (80% in 18 min) |
| Perception | rotating LiDAR + stereo cams | solid-state LiDAR + event cameras |
| Cold rating | -25°C | -30°C |
| Fleet software | VeltrixOS 4.x | VeltrixOS 5 (multi-floor support) |

## Strategic rationale

- The 950 kg payload unlocks **pallet-level handling**, moving Veltrix from
  case-picking into the larger pallet-AMR segment.
- -30°C rating extends the cold-chain moat; no competitor is certified
  below -25°C today.
- VeltrixOS 5 adds **multi-floor operation via elevator integration**, a
  frequent loss reason in RFPs against Geek+ and Locus Robotics.

## Open risks

1. Solid-state LiDAR cold-chamber certification (cycle 2 of 3 in progress;
   failure would slip launch to Q2 2027).
2. LFP pack supplier (single-sourced from Norvolt Industri) - second source
   qualification starts June 2026.
3. Pricing: sales leadership argues $56,000 is too close to the VLX-400;
   pricing committee decision due August 2026.
