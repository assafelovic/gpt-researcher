# Veltrix Dynamics B.V. - Company Overview

**Last updated: May 2026 | Internal - Confidential**

Veltrix Dynamics designs and operates autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for
warehouse and cold-chain logistics.

## Key facts

- **Founded:** 2019, Rotterdam, Netherlands
- **Headquarters:** Rotterdam (Van Nelle Campus), with offices in Atlanta and Nagoya
- **Headcount:** 241 employees as of May 2026 (up from 168 in May 2025)
- **CEO:** Mara Lindqvist (co-founder, ex-Vanderlande)
- **CTO:** Daan Verhoeven (co-founder)

## Funding history

| Round | Date | Amount | Lead investor |
|---|---|---|---|
| Seed | Mar 2020 | $4.5M | Forward.one |
| Series A | Jun 2022 | $18M | HenQ Capital |
| Series B | Feb 2024 | $34.5M | Prime Ventures |
| Series C | Oct 2025 | $85M | Meridian Growth Partners |

**Total raised: $142M.** The Series C values the company at $610M post-money.

## Business model

Two revenue lines:

1. **Hardware sales** - VLX-400 units at a list price of **$48,500** per robot.
2. **Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS)** - full-service subscription at
   **$1,950 per robot per month**, including maintenance, software and fleet
   management. RaaS now represents 58% of new bookings.

## Differentiation

Veltrix is the only AMR vendor with **EN 60068-certified cold-storage
operation down to -25°C**, making it the default choice for frozen-food
distribution centers in the EU.
