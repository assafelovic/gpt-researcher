# Board Memo: 2026 Strategy - Veltrix Dynamics

**Internal - Confidential | CEO office, January 2026**

## Where we win

Veltrix wins in **cold-chain and food logistics**: 61% of ARR comes from
frozen/chilled distribution centers. Win rate in cold-chain RFPs is 71%,
versus 24% in ambient-temperature RFPs.

## 2026 priorities

1. **Defend the cold-chain moat.** Ship the VLX-500 with -30°C rating in
   Q4 2026. Target: 80% renewal-with-expansion rate among top 20 accounts.
2. **US expansion.** Grow North America from 14 to 30 sites by year end.
   Atlanta office to double CSM headcount. Key risk: US customers demand
   UL 3100 certification; audit scheduled September 2026.
3. **Interoperability bet.** Commit to the VDA 5050 interface standard so
   VeltrixOS can orchestrate third-party robots. Board decision needed on
   whether to open VeltrixOS to competitor hardware (management recommends
   yes, gated on VLX-500 launch).

## Financial plan

- 2026 revenue target: **$96M** (2025 actual: $61M)
- Reach EBITDA breakeven by **Q3 2027**
- Cash position end of 2025: $91M; runway comfortably past breakeven
- No fundraise planned before 2027; Series D optional for M&A

## Competitive read

Management considers **Geek+ and Locus Robotics** the primary competitive
threats globally, with **Exotec** pressuring the EU grocery segment via
integrated ASRS deals. Amazon's in-house robotics is viewed as a
market-expander rather than a direct rival.
