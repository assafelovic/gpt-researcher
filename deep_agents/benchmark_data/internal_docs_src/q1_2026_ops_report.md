# Q1 2026 Operations Report - Veltrix Dynamics

**Internal - Confidential | Prepared by: COO office, April 14, 2026**

## Fleet performance

- **Deployed fleet:** 3,120 VLX-400 robots across 47 customer sites
  (EU: 29 sites, North America: 14, Japan: 4)
- **Fleet availability:** 99.2% (target: 99.0%)
- **Mean time between failures (MTBF):** 2,340 hours, up 9% QoQ
- **Average picks per robot per hour:** 142 (industry benchmark for
  comparable AMRs is roughly 100-120)

## Revenue

- **Q1 2026 revenue: $21.4M** (Q1 2025: $12.8M, +67% YoY)
- RaaS annual recurring revenue (ARR) reached **$46.9M** exiting Q1
- Gross margin: 41% blended (hardware 28%, RaaS 63%)

## Incidents

- **February 11, 2026:** firmware regression in v4.7.2 caused localization
  drift in narrow-aisle configurations; 14 sites affected, resolved within
  36 hours by rollback to v4.7.1. Root cause: LiDAR extrinsics cache
  invalidation bug. No SLA penalties triggered.

## Supply chain

- Lead time for the VLX-400 drive unit improved from 22 weeks to 14 weeks
  after qualifying a second supplier (Shimizu Precision, Nagoya).
- BOM cost per unit down 6.5% YoY to **$19,400**.

## Key risks tracked

1. Concentration: top 3 customers = 44% of ARR.
2. The planned VLX-500 launch (Q4 2026) depends on the new solid-state
   LiDAR module passing -25°C cold-chamber certification, currently in
   test cycle 2 of 3.
