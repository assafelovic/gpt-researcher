## Demand Trends in Labuan Bajo: Seasonality and Events

**Seasonality:**

- **Peak Season:**  
  - July–August (summer holidays, dry season, international tourists)
  - December–January (Christmas/New Year, domestic tourists)
  - During these periods, occupancy rates on boats and hotels can exceed 85–90%, and advance booking is essential ([LiveAboard.com](https://www.liveaboard.com/diving/indonesia/labuan-bajo)).
- **Shoulder Season:**  
  - April–June, September–October (pleasant weather, moderate demand)
- **Low Season:**  
  - February–March, November (rainy season, lowest tourist arrivals)

**Key Holidays/Events:**

- **Easter, Idul Fitri, and school holidays** see surges in domestic demand.
- **Komodo Festival** and other local events can spike short-term demand.

**Trends:**  
Labuan Bajo has seen double-digit annual growth in tourist arrivals, with a notable shift toward premium and eco-conscious travel. The market remains resilient, but oversupply of lower-tier boats is a risk during off-peak months ([Rainforest Cruises](https://www.rainforestcruises.com/cruises/bugis-yacht-indonesia)).

## Fixed and Variable Costs of Running a Luxury Yacht Charter

### Fixed Costs (Annualized/Monthly):

- **Depreciation/Financing:** $50,000–$200,000/year (varies by vessel value)
- **Insurance:** $10,000–$30,000/year
- **Licensing/Permits:** $2,000–$10,000/year
- **Maintenance (routine):** $20,000–$60,000/year
- **Crew Salaries:** $3,000–$10,000/month (for 6–12 crew)

### Variable Costs (Per Trip/Day):

- **Fuel:** $100–$1,000/day (depends on engine size, itinerary)
- **Food & Beverage:** $30–$100/guest/day (higher for private-chef service)
- **Port Fees & Park Permits:** $200–$500/trip
- **Cleaning & Consumables:** $50–$200/day

### Minimum Viable Price Calculation

Assuming a 10-guest luxury yacht with $1,000/day in fixed costs (amortized) and $600/day in variable costs:

- **Break-even daily charter rate:**  
  $1,600/day (excluding profit margin)

For premium vessels, minimum viable price is typically $2,000–$3,000/day to ensure profitability and cover contingencies ([LiveAboard.com](https://www.liveaboard.com/cruise/indonesia/bugis), [Rainforest Cruises](https://www.rainforestcruises.com/cruises/bugis-yacht-indonesia)).
