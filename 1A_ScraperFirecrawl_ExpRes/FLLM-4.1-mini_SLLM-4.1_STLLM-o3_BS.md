These complaints are minor and do not significantly detract from overall satisfaction, but they highlight areas for operational transparency and guest communication.

## 5. Demand Trends in Labuan Bajo: Seasonality and Key Events

### Seasonality

- **Peak Season:** March–December, with the driest and most popular months being June–September. This period coincides with school holidays in Australia, Europe, and Singapore, driving up demand and prices ([rainforestcruises.com](https://www.rainforestcruises.com/cruises/samara-ii-indonesia)).
- **Low Season:** January–February, when monsoon rains reduce visibility and sea conditions can be rough. Some vessels undergo maintenance during this period, reducing supply ([liveaboard.com](https://www.liveaboard.com/diving/indonesia/komodo-national-park)).
- **Demand Spikes:** Major holidays (Eid, Christmas, New Year, Chinese New Year) and special events (e.g., Komodo Festival, international diving competitions) see surges in bookings and premium pricing ([rainforestcruises.com](https://www.rainforestcruises.com/komodo-cruises)).

## 7. Fixed and Variable Costs of Running a Luxury Yacht Charter

### Fixed Costs

- **Crew Salaries:** Captain, chef, deckhands, stewards (USD 500–1,000/day for a full crew)
- **Insurance:** Hull, liability, and passenger coverage (annualized, but significant)
- **Depreciation:** Vessel wear and tear, maintenance reserve

### Variable Costs

- **Fuel:** Highly dependent on vessel size and itinerary (USD 200–800/day)
- **Food & Beverage:** Gourmet provisioning, alcohol (USD 100–400/day)
- **Port & Park Fees:** Komodo National Park (USD 120–240/trip), port charges
- **Consumables:** Water, toiletries, cleaning supplies

### Minimum Viable Price Calculation

For a mid-size luxury phinisi (e.g., Samara II):

- Crew: $700/day
- Fuel: $300/day
- F&B: $250/day
- Park/Port Fees: $50/day (averaged)
- Maintenance/Depreciation: $250/day

**Total Minimum Operating Cost:** ~$1,550/day

**Break-Even Price:** To ensure profitability and cover booking commissions, the minimum viable charter price is typically set at 2x operating cost, i.e., **$3,000/day** ([rainforestcruises.com](https://www.rainforestcruises.com/cruises/samara-ii-indonesia)).
