## 7. **Fixed and Variable Costs of Running a Luxury Yacht Charter**

### **Fixed Costs**
- **Depreciation/Financing**: Annualized cost of vessel acquisition or lease.
- **Insurance**: Hull, liability, and crew insurance (annual, apportioned daily).
- **Crew Salaries**: Captain, chef, deckhands, stewards (monthly, apportioned daily).
- **Maintenance**: Routine servicing, dry-docking, and upgrades.

### **Variable Costs**
- **Fuel**: Highly dependent on itinerary and vessel type.
- **Provisions**: Food, beverages, and guest-specific requests.
- **Park Fees & Permits**: Komodo National Park entry and activity fees.
- **Utilities**: Water, electricity (generator diesel), waste disposal.
- **Marketing/Agency Commissions**: For each booking.

### **Minimum Viable Price Calculation**
For a mid-size luxury phinisi (8–12 guests), the break-even day-rate typically falls between **$1,500–$2,000/day**, factoring in all fixed and variable costs, with a 10–15% margin for unforeseen expenses ([Rainforest Cruises](https://www.rainforestcruises.com/cruises/augustine-phinisi-indonesia?charter=true); [Boatcrowd](https://www.boatcrowd.com/yacht-charter-labuan-bajo)).
